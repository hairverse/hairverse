1. What is Hair and Its Importance | कपाल के हो?

Hair is a part of our body made of a protein called keratin. It grows from the root under the skin in a tube called a follicle. Hair is not just for style — it also has many important roles.

Importance of Hair:

  • Protection: Hair protects our scalp from sunlight, dust, and pollution.
  • Temperature control: It helps to keep the head warm in winter.
  • Beauty: Hair is an important part of our appearance and personality.

कपाल हाम्रो शरीरको एक भाग हो जुन केराटिन भन्ने प्रोटिनबाट बनेको हुन्छ। यो छालाभित्रको जराबाट उम्रिन्छ। कपाल केवल शोभा होइन, यसको उपयोगिता पनि धेरै छ।

कपालको महत्व:

  • सुरक्षा: कपालले टाउकोलाई घाम, धुलो, र प्रदूषणबाट जोगाउँछ।
  • तातोपन नियन्त्रण: जाडोमा टाउकोलाई न्यानो राख्न मद्दत गर्छ।
  • सौन्दर्य: कपाल हाम्रो सुन्दरता र व्यक्तित्वको भाग हो।

2. Types of Hair | कपालका प्रकारहरू

Not everyone has the same type of hair. Knowing your hair type helps you take better care of it.

  • Straight Hair (सिधा कपाल): This hair type is flat and smooth. It is often shiny but can become oily quickly.
    सिधा कपाल सीधा र चम्किलो देखिन्छ, तर छिट्टै चिल्लो हुनसक्छ।
  • Wavy Hair (तरंगिएको कपाल): It has gentle waves in "S" shape. It is between straight and curly.
    यसमा S आकारको तरंग हुन्छ। न त सीधा, न त धेरै घुमिएको।
  • Curly Hair (घुमिएको कपाल): Curls are tight or spiral. It looks voluminous and is often dry.
    घुमिएको कपाल प्राय: सुक्खा हुन्छ र घुम्टिएर आउने गर्छ।

3. Ways of Caring Hair | कपाल जोगाउने तरिकाहरू

Hair care is important to keep your hair healthy, strong, and beautiful.

  • Use a gentle shampoo: Wash your hair with a mild shampoo twice or thrice a week.
    हल्का श्याम्पु प्रयोग गरी हप्ता २-३ पटक कपाल धोउनुस्।
  • Oil your hair: Apply natural oil like coconut or mustard oil.
    नारिवल वा तोरीको तेल लगाउनु लाभदायक हुन्छ।
  • Avoid too much heat: Don’t use dryers or straighteners too often.
    ड्रायर वा स्ट्रेटनर बढी प्रयोग नगर्नुस्।
  • Eat healthy food: Include fruits, vegetables, and proteins in your diet.
    फलफूल, सागसब्जी, प्रोटिनयुक्त खाना खाँदा कपाल बलियो हुन्छ।
  • Trim regularly: Trim your hair every few months to remove split ends.
    फाटेका पुछार हटाउन २-३ महिनामा एकपटक काट्नुस्।
